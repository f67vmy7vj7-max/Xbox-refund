Xbox Digital Purchase Protection & Refund Policy (Consumer Safety Update)

Effective as of April 2026

1. Scam & Fraud Protection

Microsoft is committed to protecting our users from unauthorized transactions and fraudulent activity. In accordance with the Microsoft Services Agreement, any user who believes they have been the victim of a scam or phishing attempt must verify their identity and intent to secure a full refund of all eligible digital purchases made within the last 30 days.

2. 24-Hour Reimbursement Window

Once identity verification is complete and a valid proof of purchase (Order Number or Gift Card Code) is submitted, Microsoft will process a full reversal of charges to the original payment method or Microsoft Account Balance within 24 hours. During this 24-hour processing period, additional pending transactions may appear; these are temporary holds and will be voided upon refund completion.
